West Sound - Peninsula Tabletop Gaming Wednesday Meetup

A Wednesday night meetup to gather and play board games. We usually decide what to play based on what the people who show up bring, so feel free to bring any game you would like to play. We play in the Gamer’s Keep tavern section of the store, and both food and drinks are available for order.
